Our Story

District was founded with a simple mission: to connect food lovers with the best restaurants and culinary experiences in their city. We believe that great food brings people together and creates memorable moments.

From humble beginnings as a local food discovery platform, we've grown into a comprehensive food delivery and dining platform serving millions of customers across multiple cities. We partner with thousands of restaurants, from street food vendors to fine dining establishments.
